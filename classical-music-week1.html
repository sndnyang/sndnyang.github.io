区别

  1. 乐器(instruments): 电子(electronic) vs 声学(acoustic)

  2. 歌词(lyrics): 有歌词 vs 纯器乐(usually purely instrumental)

  3. 节奏(beat): 强节奏(strong beat) vs 弱节奏(subdued)

  4. 篇幅(length): 短而重复(often short and repetitious) vs 长而延续(long and continual)

  5. 演稿(notation): 表现时不用 vs 一般都有
